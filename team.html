Thomas Butera

Managing Director

Thomas Butera is a seasoned real estate professional with over 25 years of industry experience, currently serving as Managing Director at Englemount Real Estate Management Services Corp. Based in Toronto, Ontario, Tom has established a reputation for delivering exceptional results — earning recognition both locally and nationally in property management and leasing.

His career is marked by a dedication to maximizing value for both institutional and private owners through strategic planning and disciplined execution. Tom is known for meticulous attention to detail and a client-centric approach that ensures long-term solutions benefiting owners and occupants alike.

Christopher Tsugawa, CPA, CGA

Vice President, Finance

Christopher Tsugawa is an experienced and respected financial executive in the real estate management sector, currently serving as Vice President of Finance at Englemount. With over 25 years of expertise in financial management, accounting, and strategic fiscal planning, Christopher plays a pivotal role in guiding the company's financial strategy.

He oversees corporate governance, risk management, technology, and financial operations. His background includes property management accounting, financial statement audits, business valuation, and compliance — ensuring the financial health and operational excellence of Englemount.

Alvaro Gramolini

Director of Property Management

Alvaro Gramolini is an experienced property management professional with 20 years of experience. As Director of Property Management at Englemount, Alvaro has demonstrated a profound ability to manage diverse property portfolios — including commercial, industrial, and residential sectors.
